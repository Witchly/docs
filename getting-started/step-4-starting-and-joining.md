To join the game, you need your "Server Address." Look at the top right corner (or under the server name). You will see a button that says Address or IP.

#### ğŸ® 4. How to Join the Game

For Minecraft Players:

1. Open Minecraft on your PC.
2. Click Multiplayer.
3. Click Add Server.
4. Server Name: Type anything (e.g., "My Witchly Server").
5. Server Address: Paste the IP you just copied.
6. Click Done and join!

For Hytale / Other Games:

1. Open your game.
2. Find the "Connect to Server" or "Direct Connect" button.
3. Paste the IP you copied.
4. Click Connect.
