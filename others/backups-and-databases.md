Never lose your progress. Witchly allows you to create instant snapshots of your server.

How to Create:

1. Go to the "Backups" tab.
2. Click Create Backup.
3. Name it: (e.g., "Before Update").
4. Ignored Files: Leave this blank to backup _everything_.
5. Click Start Backup.

How to Restore:

* Messed something up? Just click the three dots (...) next to a backup and choose Restore.
